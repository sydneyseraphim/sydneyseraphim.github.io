Projects

These are some of the things I've worked on over the last couple of years.

Lalo the Autonomous Robot
Lalo is an autonomous robot that I created with three other students for the 2017 Engineering Physics robot competition. I was mostly responsible for the mechanical design and modelling, which was done in OnShape. I also constructed several of the circuits and contributed to the code. You can learn more about what Lalo can do at the project's website.
Skills: OnShape, Arduino/C.
Fabrication: soldering, water-jet cutting, laser cutting, shop tools.
Tabs
This year I attended my first hackathon along with a team including three other engineering physics students. Together, we created an app called "Tabs" that allows friends to easily keep track of IOUs.

The app was built in a 36-hour time frame and won Best Design at HackPrinceton (Fall 2017).

Skills: ReactNative.
